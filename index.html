How Permetrax works
1
Deterministic rule engine. Plain code compares your design to the jurisdiction's numeric limits and decides every pass/fail. No AI touches the math — results are reproducible.
2
Explanation layer. The engine's output is turned into plain-English findings and fix suggestions (built-in, or live Claude if you add a key). The AI explains — it never changes a verdict or invents a rule.
3
Report & save. See a scored dashboard with a site-plan diagram, export a PDF, and save projects in your browser.
Why split it this way? Compliance must be auditable. Letting an LLM "decide" zoning would be unreliable and unexplainable — so the LLM is confined to the one thing it's great at: explaining.
